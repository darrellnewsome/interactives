The Loop of Cloud Computing

1. Devices

The "Entry Point". Phones and laptops show results but don't do the heavy lifting.

2. The Internet

The "Bridge". Without a connection, your device cannot reach the "brain" of the app.

3. Data Centers

The "Physical Cloud". Massive buildings full of powerful servers running 24/7.

💡 Key Concept:

"Cloud apps usually require Wi-Fi or mobile data because the 'brain' of the app lives somewhere else."

The Three Layers: SaaS, PaaS, IaaS

SaaS (Software as a Service)

Target: Everyone (User-focused)

You just use the app. Provider handles everything. (Google Docs, Netflix, Spotify)

PaaS (Platform as a Service)

Target: Developers (Creator-focused)

You provide code; they provide tools and platform. (Google App Engine)

IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service)

Target: IT Professionals (Admin-focused)

You rent virtual hardware and manage the OS. (AWS, Azure)
