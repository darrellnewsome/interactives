Email Revision Challenge

Professional communication is a key skill. In this activity, you will evaluate poor communication habits and practice crafting a professional alternative.

PART 1

Evaluate (10 Minutes)

Read these three unprofessional emails. Identify the errors and explain why they fail to meet professional standards.

EMAIL CASE STUDY A
From: skaterboy99@email.com Subject: (No Subject) hey i wont be in class today cuz i got stuff to do. can u send me what we did. thx.
EMAIL CASE STUDY B
From: sarah.j@school.edu Subject: URGENT HELP ME NOW!!!!! I DONT GET THE ASSIGNMENT AND IT IS DUE IN AN HOUR WHY IS IT SO HARD? I NEED HELP RIGHT NOW PLEASE EMAIL ME BACK ASAP!!!!!!!!
EMAIL CASE STUDY C
From: k_smith@email.com Subject: Question Yo, Mr. Miller. Did you grade my test yet? I need to know my grade before practice. Let me know.
PART 2

Rewrite (10 Minutes)

Choose ONE of the emails from above and rewrite it completely in a professional format.
