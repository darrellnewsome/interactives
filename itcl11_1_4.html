Independent Activity – Cloud in Your Daily Life

📖 Key Concepts

Cloud computing is the on-demand delivery of computing power, database storage, and apps over the internet. It's like "renting" space from a giant remote computer.

Real-World Fact:

Services live in Data Centers—buildings the size of football fields filled with thousands of servers.

Local vs. Cloud

  • 💾 Local: Internal drives, USB sticks. Risk: Device lost = data lost.
  • ☁️ Cloud: Data centers, internet required. Benefit: Data stays safe even if hardware breaks.
